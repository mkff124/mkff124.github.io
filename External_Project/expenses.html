Where Your Money Goes

We have an amazing group of staff members working in the background, In order to be able to keep the organization running 24/7 we rely on sponsors. Your contribution helps us with water, electricity and other expenses such as farriers. One major expense is medical, When we rescue abused horses they usually need urgent medical attention.
